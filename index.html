A little about me

I'm aspiring software developer with a passion for solving problems and learning new things. My journey to become a software developer has been filled with courage and faith. Since graduating college, I have had trouble finding a career that has intrigued and challenged me. I was looking to further my education but was afraid to enter the technology field because of my limited background.
It wasn't until I got engaged when I realized that I needed to make a decision to persue software development. I choose to spend the wedding money my parents have me to attend this school. It was a bold move I made giving up my wedding to further my education but so far it has been when of the best decisions of my life. NSS has been the most challenging and humbling experience I have ever been through. Looking back at all of learned in such a short period of time has given me my curiosity back.
I have primarily worked in sales since moving to Nashville back in 2016. I was a realtor and currently am a patient enrollment specialist at a health care company. I am eager to use my customer service and communication skills to and bring a new element software development.
I am looking for a career that keeps me on my toes and gives me the opportunity to grow and learn. I enjoy solving problems. I have always been intrigued as to how much technology has changed and helps billions of peoples lives.
Through virtual schools to connecting families on social media technology is changing the way we think and do. I want to be a part of the industrial revolution and contribute to the change and evolution of the world.
